About

Artwork, Generated by a Machine

The Machine is made up of 1's and 0's. It looks out on the world. It absorbs what it sees, and uses it to create art.

The machine is a piece of software. Lines of 1's and 0's. Invidually they don't amount to much. Just as a lone cell in the human body doesn't amount to much. But when combined it provides the machine with the ability to observe. To monitor the ebbs and flows of the physical and digital world through data.

The machine absorbs data, feeds on it, and uses it to create its own artwork. The machine is just a piece of software. Composed of 1's and 0's. A reflection of reality.

The machine chooses its best artwork, mints it as an NFT, and makes it available for purchase on Super Rare. Any income produced from the sale of its NFT artwork is used keep the machine powered up and computing. The machine feeds on data, which it uses to create art, so it can continue feeding.
